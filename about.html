WHO WE ARE.

The Girl Child Foundation is a nonprofit Organization.Our mission is to help the girl child,in hearing their voices in every aspect of life.We give hope and encourage the girl child to work hard in everything they do.We are partnering with various donors and partners to make a common goal of helping the girl child. We are based in adlife plaza along Arwings Kodhek road.

Come join us and watch your life change instantly!

OUR STORY.

The Girl Child Foundation was founded in January 2020 by Mercy Vugutsa.We play a major role in helping the girls to overcome the various challenges that are affecting them physically,mentally and emotionally.We make the girls participate in activities that are beneficial to them in their daily lives.So far we only work in helping girls within Nairobi County but hopefully we will expand to more counties as time goes by.
